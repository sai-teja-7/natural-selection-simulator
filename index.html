Simulation Core Rules

  • Foraging Rules: Creatures emerge from the edges every morning to search for green energy pellets.
  • Natural Selection:
    • 0 Pellets: Blob starves and dies.
    • 1 Pellet: Blob survives to the next day.
    • 2 Pellets: Blob clones itself, producing an offspring with mutated traits.
  • Speed Color Spectrum: Blob colors represent their Speed trait across a continuous spectrum matching Primer's scale:
    • Pink/Purple: Slowest (~0.5x speed)
    • Teal/Blue: Average (~1.0x speed)
    • Orange/Yellow: Fast (~1.5x speed)
    • Red: Fastest (2.0x+ speed)
  • Speed Mechanic: Faster acceleration lets blobs beat others to food but uses exponential energy proportional to Speed².
  • Size Mechanic: Blobs at least 20% larger can aggressively hunt and eat smaller agents for meat. Size heavily escalates stationary energy drain as Size³.
  • Sense Mechanic: Greater vision radius grants long-range targeting efficiency but charges a permanent base tax matching the field range value.
